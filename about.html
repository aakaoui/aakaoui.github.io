About Me

Data Analyst | Digital Marketing | Market Research

Hi, I'm Alex - a data / digital marketing analyst. Curious about the "/"? Let me explain.
Having worked in market research and digital marketing for a few years, I was open and ready for the next right step - a step that combines my industry experience with my passion to work with data.
I enrolled into the Data Analytics program at the London School of Economics and here I am after six grueling months, beaming with pride after completing it.
In the program, we not only learned to code with different languages (Python, SQL and R) and build dashboards - we learned how to uncover the untold stories of raw data.
What I really enjoy doing is peeling back the layers of a dataset, uncovering insights, and creating dashboards that help drive the business forward.
I am committed to lifelong learning and eager to see where my next step takes me.
