About Me

Data Analyst | Digital Marketing | Market Research

Hi, I'm Alex - a data / digital marketing analyst. Curious about the "/"? Let me explain.
Having worked in market research and digital marketing for a few years, I was looking for the next right step for me - a step that combines my industry experience with my passion to work with data. I enrolled into the data analytics program at the London School of Economics and here I am after six grueling months, beaming with pride after completing it. In the program, we not only learned to code with different languages (Python, SQL and R) and build dashbards - we learned how to transform raw data into actionable stories. What I really enjoy doing is peeling back the layers of a dataset, uncovering insights, and creating dashboards that help drive the business forward. I am committed to lifelong learning and eager to see where my next step takes me.
